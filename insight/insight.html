FAQ

I already have an app for this, what's so different about this? When you log into a third-party service with your username and password, you don't know what sort of malicious activities could be happening behind the scenes. You also risk having your account being suspended or banned through their services.

What data are you storing? Privacy is something we really value and with Insight we promise no usernames, passwords, or data are stored outside of this website.

What does open-source mean? Insight is an open-source project, where anyone can access and review the code. The reason we want this project to be open-sourced is to build trust with you all!
